5 Tips for Stunning Photos

Home Articles

1. Lighting: Always shoot with natural light when possible. Golden hour (sunrise & sunset) works best.

2. Angles: Don’t always shoot at eye level. Try higher or lower angles for creative shots.

3. Background: Keep it clean. Remove distractions so the subject stands out.

4. Editing: Use presets or Lightroom to enhance colors but don’t overdo it.

5. Consistency: Keep your style uniform if you want a professional portfolio.
